Created by @martinjc for no other reason than he had an Untappd API key that wasn't getting used. Find him on Untappd here and on a bit of the internet here. Some of the beers were drunk by him, this much is almost guaranteed.

All data comes from Untappd's 'local' feed, so only includes beer checkins that have a venue attached. Data is updated every ten minutes. Want to influence these stats? Sign up for Untappd and checkin your beers! Want to influence these stats but not give away your location? Try checking in with your neighbourhood as the venue.
